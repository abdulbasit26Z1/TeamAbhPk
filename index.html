Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-4 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages depending on your needs. All projects include a warranty period, and we can provide ongoing maintenance, updates, and technical support.

Can you work with our existing design or development team?

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams, other agencies, or freelancers. We're flexible and can adapt to your preferred workflow.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with a wide range of technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, and various databases. We choose the best technology stack for each project's specific requirements.
