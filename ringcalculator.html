How It Works

1

Select Numbers on the Ring

Simply tap or swipe around the circular ring interface to select numbers. The ring is divided into segments for easy number selection.

Select mathematical operations (+, -, ×, ÷) by tapping the corresponding buttons around the ring or using gesture controls.

Watch as calculations appear instantly on the display. The ring provides haptic feedback for each input, confirming your selections.
