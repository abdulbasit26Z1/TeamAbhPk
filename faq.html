Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and company policies.

General Questions

What types of software solutions does TechEra offer?

TechEra provides a wide range of software solutions including custom business applications, cloud-based SaaS products, mobile apps, enterprise software, and AI-powered tools. Our solutions are tailored to meet specific industry needs across healthcare, finance, education, and more.

How can I request a demo of your products?

You can request a demo by visiting our Apps page and clicking the "Request Demo" button on any product, or by contacting our sales team through our Contact page. We typically respond to demo requests within 1 business day.

What industries do you specialize in?

While our solutions are adaptable across industries, we have particular expertise in:

  • Financial services and fintech
  • Healthcare and medical technology
  • Education and e-learning platforms
  • Retail and e-commerce
  • Manufacturing and logistics

Technical Support

What are your system requirements?

System requirements vary by product. Most of our web applications work on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with JavaScript enabled. Our desktop applications typically require:

  • Windows 10/11 or macOS 10.15+
  • 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended)
  • 2GHz dual-core processor
  • 500MB disk space

Specific requirements are listed on each product's documentation page.

How do I get technical support for your products?

We offer multiple support channels:

  • Email: support@techera.example.com (response within 24 hours)
  • Phone: 1-800-TECH-ERA (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST)
  • Live Chat: Available through our web portal during business hours
  • Knowledge Base: Comprehensive documentation and troubleshooting guides

Enterprise clients have access to priority 24/7 support.

Do you provide API access to your platforms?

Yes, most of our enterprise products include robust API access. We provide:

  • Comprehensive API documentation
  • Sample code in multiple languages
  • Developer sandbox environments
  • Technical support for integration

API availability varies by product tier - please check the specific product details or contact our sales team for more information.

Pricing & Licensing

What pricing models do you offer?

We offer flexible pricing options to suit different needs:

  • Subscription: Monthly or annual SaaS pricing with tiered features
  • Perpetual License: One-time purchase for on-premise solutions
  • Usage-Based: Pay-as-you-go for some cloud services
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large deployments

Most products offer free trials or demo versions. Visit our Apps page for specific pricing details.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we provide special pricing for:

  • Registered non-profit organizations (50% discount)
  • Educational institutions (40% discount)
  • Startups in accelerator programs (30% discount for first year)

Please contact our sales team with proof of eligibility to access these discounts.

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy varies by product:

  • SaaS Subscriptions: Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase
  • Perpetual Licenses: 90-day money-back guarantee
  • Custom Development: Milestone-based payments with cancellation terms outlined in contract

Refunds are processed within 10 business days. Some restrictions may apply - please see our Terms of Service for complete details.
